Tourism

Chitral is famous for its touristic attractions. Starting from the Kalash Valley to the Chitral Gol National Park, there is great diversity for tourists with different tastes. There are those that come to trek the deadly Terich Mir and then there are those that want to escape the busy metropolitan areas and camp in the lush green hills of Birmoghlasht for the weekend. Wild Life

Have you ever heard of a Markhor? Well if you haven't, it is the name of the majestic goat you see on the left. Known also as the "screw-horn goat", the Markhor is the National Animal of Pakistan. It has also been classified as a "Near Threatened" and placed on the IUCN Red List. In Chitral, one can enjoy these goats upto a proximity of 10 meters when they come down from the mountain peaks to drink water right before the sun sets over the HinduKush Range.
